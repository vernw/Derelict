printed name: "Bridge" 

description: "The bridge is where the ship is commanded and piloted from. It has a large bay window facing into the abyss of space, though it's usually covered by much more useful projection screens. It is situated quite like a conference room, with the Captain's chair at the head of an elliptical table facing the window, with the various department heads having their designated seats around the long sides.
